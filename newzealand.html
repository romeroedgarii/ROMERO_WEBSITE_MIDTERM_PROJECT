New Zealand





Why Visit ?

New Zealand is a breathtaking paradise known for its stunning landscapes, outdoor adventures, and rich Māori culture. Ever since my auntie moved to New Zealand, it has always been a dream of mine to visit her there and see how beautiful New Zealand is. From snow-capped mountains and pristine beaches to glowworm caves and lush forests, this island nation is a dream destination for nature lovers, thrill-seekers, and movie fans (especially Lord of the Rings fans!).


Auckland

Auckland is New Zealand’s largest city and a hub of culture, food, and adventure. Surrounded by volcanic islands, beaches, and lush parks, it offers a perfect mix of urban and natural experiences.

How to get here

Fly into Auckland International Airport (AKL).










Hobbiton

Hobbiton is one of the most magical destinations in New Zealand, especially for fans of The Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit trilogies. Located in the rolling green hills of Matamata, this real-life movie set brings J.R.R. Tolkien’s Shire to life.

How to get here

A 2-hour drive from Auckland










Queenstown

Queenstown is New Zealand’s top adventure destination, surrounded by majestic mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and endless outdoor activities. It’s perfect for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike!

How to get here

Fly into Queenstown Airport (ZQN)
