Paris

Paris is one of the most iconic and romantic cities in the world, known for its stunning architecture, rich history, world-class museums, and vibrant culture. Whether you’re visiting for the art, food, fashion, or romance, Paris has something magical to offer.

How to get here

Fly into Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG).

Other places to visit in Paris

Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe

The Champs-Élysées is one of the most famous avenues in the world, stretching 1.9 km (1.2 miles) from the Place de la Concorde to the Arc de Triomphe.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre is the largest and most visited museum in the world, housing over 35,000 artworks, including Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and The Winged Victory of Samothrace.










Versailles – The Grand Palace

The Palace of Versailles is one of the most magnificent royal residences in the world. Once home to King Louis XIV, the "Sun King," this is a masterpiece of French Baroque architecture, art, and landscape design.

How to get here

A 30-minute train from Paris.









French Riviera

The French Riviera (Côte d’Azur) is a sun-soaked Mediterranean paradise known for its luxury, history, and breathtaking coastal landscapes. From glamorous cities like Nice, Cannes, and Monaco to charming villages and crystal-clear beaches, the Riviera offers a mix of culture, nature, and elegance.

How to get here

Fly into Nice Côte d'Azur Airport (NCE).
