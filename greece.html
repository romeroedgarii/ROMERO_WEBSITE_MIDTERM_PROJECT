Greece





Why Visit ?

As a previous Architecture Student, Greece will definitely be my top choice to visit. Greece is home to ancient ruins, charming whitewashed islands, and some of the most breathtaking coastlines in the world. Whether you’re into history, food, or beach-hopping, Greece has it all.


Athens

Athens, the historic capital of Greece, is a city where ancient history meets modern vibrancy. As the birthplace of democracy, philosophy, and classical art, Athens is filled with iconic landmarks, stunning ruins, lively streets, and delicious Mediterranean cuisine.

How to get here

Fly into Athens International Airport (ATH)

Other places to visit in Athens

Acropolis & Parthenon

The most famous ancient Greek site.

Plaka Neighborhood

Explore charming streets with cafes and shops.










Santorini

Santorini is one of the most famous islands in the world, known for its breathtaking sunsets, white-washed buildings with blue domes, volcanic landscapes, and crystal-clear waters.

How to get here

A 45-minute flight from Athens or a ferry ride

Other places to visit in Santorini

Oia & Fira

The most famous village in Santorini, known for its stunning sunset views. Features blue-domed churches, narrow alleys, and luxury cave hotels.

Red Beach & Black Sand Beach

Santorini’s unique volcanic landscape has created some of the most extraordinary beaches in the world.










Mykonos

Mykonos is one of the most famous Greek islands, known for its cosmopolitan vibe, stunning beaches, iconic windmills, vibrant nightlife, and picturesque whitewashed houses. It’s a top destination for luxury travelers, beach lovers, partygoers, and anyone looking for a mix of relaxation and adventure.

How to get here

A 45-minute flight from Athens or a ferry ride.

Other places to visit in Mykonos

Little Venice & Windmills

One of the most picturesque areas in Greece, with Venetian-style houses built right on the water’s edge.

Paradise Beach

One of the most famous beaches in Greece, known for crystal-clear waters, golden sand, and vibrant beach parties.
