Bucket List





For me, my bucket list is like a map of my soul’s longings—a mix of childhood dreams, spontaneous desires, and things I never knew I wanted until I saw them in a movie, read about them in a book, or felt a spark of curiosity. It’s about the places that call to me, the adventures that excite me, and the connections I want to make with the world.


List I've Done

✅ Take a Stroll Around Venice, Italy

Wandering through the canals of Venice, crossing charming bridges, and seeing gondolas glide by felt like stepping into a storybook. The Piazza San Marco and the Rialto Bridge were breathtaking, and getting lost in the narrow streets was part of the magic.



✅ Visit the Red Square in Moscow, Russia

Standing in front of Saint Basil’s Cathedral with its colorful onion domes felt surreal. The history of Lenin’s Mausoleum and the grandeur of the Kremlin made this experience unforgettable. The energy of the Red Square was both overwhelming and awe-inspiring.



✅ See St. Petersburg, Russia

The city felt like an open-air museum, with grand palaces like the Winter Palace and the breathtaking Church of the Savior on Spilled Blood. Walking along the Neva River and visiting the Hermitage Museum was like traveling back in time to imperial Russia.



✅ Perform Internationally

Music has always been a big part of my life, and getting the chance to perform on an international stage was a dream come true. The thrill of sharing my passion with audiences from different cultures was an unforgettable experience that reinforced my love for music.










List I Want to Do

⬜ See the Northern Lights in Iceland

Watching the Aurora Borealis dance across the Icelandic sky would be a once-in-a-lifetime experience. There's something magical about witnessing nature’s light show in the freezing Arctic air.



⬜ Ride a Hot Air Balloon in Cappadocia, Turkey

Floating above Cappadocia’s fairy chimneys and surreal landscapes at sunrise is one of the most breathtaking views I can imagine. The mix of adventure and beauty makes it a dream experience.



⬜ Sleep Under a Tree in a Field in Europe

There’s something incredibly peaceful about the idea of lying under a massive tree in a quiet European countryside, feeling the breeze and listening to nature. A simple yet profound way to connect with the world.



⬜ Hug a Sequoia Tree in California, USA

These ancient giants have stood for thousands of years, and just being in their presence would be humbling. Hugging one would be my way of feeling connected to history, nature, and something greater than myself.





⬜ Stargaze at Mauna Kea, Hawaii, USA

Mauna Kea is home to some of the clearest night skies in the world. Watching the stars from above the clouds at nearly 14,000 feet would be a deeply spiritual and awe-inspiring experience.
