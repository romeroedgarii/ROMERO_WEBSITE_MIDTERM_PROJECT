Maldives





Why Visit ?

The Maldives is a dream destination of mine as it has so many pristine white beaches, crystal-clear waters, and luxurious overwater villas. It’s the perfect escape for relaxation, water adventures, and romantic getaways which I know the Philippines has, but it's always fun to explore new places.


Malé

Malé, the capital city of the Maldives, is a bustling urban hub amidst the country’s world-famous islands. Unlike the tranquil overwater bungalows and white sandy beaches of the Maldivian resorts, Malé offers a vibrant blend of culture, history, and local life.

How to get here

Land at Velana International Airport (MLE) in Malé.

Other places to visit in Malé

Grand Friday Mosque

The largest and most significant mosque in the Maldives, known for its stunning white marble architecture, golden dome, and peaceful atmosphere.

Malé Local Markets

Malé, the bustling capital of the Maldives, offers a vibrant local market scene where you can experience authentic Maldivian life.










Vaadhoo Island

Vaadhoo Island, part of the Raa Atoll in the Maldives, is a small yet stunning tropical paradise famous for its bioluminescent beach, known as the Sea of Stars.

How to get here

After landing at Velana International Airport (MLE) in Malé, you can ride a speedboat for 15 minutes.









Ari Atoll

Ari Atoll is one of the largest and most beautiful atolls in the Maldives, famous for its crystal-clear waters, stunning white-sand beaches, and world-class diving sites, home to some of the best luxury resorts, coral reefs, and marine life.

How to get here

A 30-minute seaplane ride from Malé
