Japan





Why Visit ?

As someone who has indulged in Japan's culture ever since I was a child, it has always been a dream of mine to visit Japan. This amazing country offers a rich cultural heritage, cutting-edge technology, and stunning natural landscapes. From the ancient shrines of Kyoto to the futuristic skyline of Tokyo, every corner of Japan is worth exploring.


Tokyo

Tokyo is a city like no other—a futuristic metropolis where ancient temples coexist with neon skyscrapers, and cutting-edge technology blends with timeless traditions.

How to get here

Land at Narita (NRT) or Haneda (HND) Airport. Take the Narita Express or Tokyo Monorail to the city.

Other places to visit in Tokyo

Shibuya Crossing

The world’s busiest pedestrian crossing.

Shinjuku & Akihabara

A part of Tokyo that is perfect for anime, gaming, and shopping.










Kyoto

Kyoto is the heart of traditional Japan, known for its centuries-old temples, stunning gardens, tea houses, and preserved geisha culture.

How to get here

Take the Shinkansen (bullet train) from Tokyo (2.5 hours).

Other places to visit in Kyoto

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Famous for its thousands of red torii gates.

Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)

A stunning gold-covered temple.










Osaka

Osaka is Japan’s vibrant, food-loving, and fun-filled city! Known as the "Nation’s Kitchen" (Tenka no Daidokoro), it is a paradise for food lovers, culture seekers, and thrill-seekers.

How to get here

By also a Shinkansen ride from Tokyo (2.5 hours).

Other places to visit in Osaka

Dotonbori

A lively street with famous food spots.

Osaka Castle

A beautiful historical landmark that is a symbol of power, resilience, and history.
